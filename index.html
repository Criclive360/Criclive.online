IPL 2026: The Complete Guide to Live Scores, Teams, Schedule and Fantasy Tips

📅 March 28, 2026 ✍️ CricLive Editorial ⏱️ 10 min read

IPL 2026 is the most anticipated cricket event of the year, and CricLive is your real-time companion for every ball bowled. Ball-by-ball live scores, breaking news, match analysis, and expert fantasy guidance — all on one clean, fast platform.

🏆 Season at a Glance

  • Start Date: March 28, 2026
  • Total Matches: 84 league + playoff matches
  • Format: Double round-robin, top 4 qualify for playoffs
  • Prize Money: ₹20 Crore for the winner
  • Broadcast: Star Sports (TV), JioCinema (free streaming)

🏏 All 10 IPL 2026 Teams

🔵 Mumbai Indians — Wankhede Stadium

Five-time champions under Rohit Sharma. The most successful franchise in IPL history with a batting lineup built for the Wankhede pitch.

🟡 Chennai Super Kings — MA Chidambaram Stadium

MS Dhoni's franchise. Four titles, ten finals — the gold standard of IPL consistency. Chepauk's spin-friendly surface is purpose-built for their bowling attack.

🔴 Royal Challengers Bengaluru — Chinnaswamy Stadium

Virat Kohli's side with cricket's most passionate fanbase. The flat Chinnaswamy pitch consistently produces 200+ scores.

🟣 Kolkata Knight Riders — Eden Gardens

Two-time champions at India's most iconic ground. KKR combine flair with tactical intelligence.

🔵 Gujarat Titans — Narendra Modi Stadium

Back-to-back title winners in their debut seasons. Playing at the world's largest cricket stadium gives them a formidable home advantage.

🟠 Sunrisers Hyderabad — Rajiv Gandhi International Stadium

2016 champions who have reinvented themselves as T20's most aggressive batting unit — their record-breaking team totals have redefined what is possible in 20 overs.

🔴 Rajasthan Royals — Sawai Mansingh Stadium

The inaugural 2008 IPL champions with a reputation for identifying and developing young talent before anyone else.

🔵 Delhi Capitals — Arun Jaitley Stadium

Building around young Indian talent. DC have the potential to peak at exactly the right time in the 2026 playoff stages.

🔴 Punjab Kings — IS Bindra Stadium

Hard-hitting batting culture at Mohali. PBKS are overdue for a serious title challenge.

🟢 Lucknow Super Giants — Ekana Cricket Stadium

Consistent playoff qualifiers since their debut in 2022. LSG have built a well-balanced squad suited to multiple conditions.
